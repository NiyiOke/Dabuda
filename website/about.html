About the NIN–Credit Integration

Nigeria faces a significant credit gap, hindering the growth of individuals and businesses. By anchoring credit records to the National Identification Number (NIN), we are laying the foundation for a trusted credit system accessible to all citizens.

Timeline of Reforms (2007–2025+)

  • 2007: Launch of NIN program
  • 2018: Digital identity reforms
  • 2022: AI-driven credit scoring pilots
  • 2024: National rollout of NIN-based credit integration
  • 2025+: Expansion and continuous improvement
